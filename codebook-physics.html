Compton wavelength of electron = 2^77 Planck lengths 电子康普顿波长 = 2^77 普朗克长度

The Compton wavelength (0.306) of an electron (0.21) equals 2^77 Planck lengths. This relationship is measurable by any civilization — they can verify this ratio independently. The exponent 77 in binary is 1001101. 电子(0.21)的康普顿波长(0.306)等于2^77个普朗克长度。 这个关系是任何文明都可以测量的——他们可以独立验证这个比值。 指数77的二进制是1001101。

Electron mass ≈ 2^(-74) Planck mass 电子质量 ≈ 2的-74次方 普朗克质量

The electron (1 particle with 1 electron, 0 protons, 0 neutrons) has a mass approximately equal to 2^(-74) Planck masses. The symbol 1.001 indicates "approximately equal" (≈), and 0.12 is subtraction, so "0.16 0.12 1001010" means "^ minus 74" = "^(-74)". The exponent 74 in binary is 1001010. 电子（1个粒子，1个电子，0个质子，0个中子）的质量约等于2^(-74)普朗克质量。 符号1.001表示"约等于"(≈)，0.12是减法，所以"0.16 0.12 1001010"意为"^减74"="^(-74)"。 指数74的二进制是1001010。

Reduced Planck Constant

The Quantum of Action ℏ = 0.303

Teaching Strategy: ℏ is defined through its relationship with other Planck units. 教学策略：ℏ通过与其他普朗克单位的关系来定义。

0.303 0.5 0.15 0.5 0.33 0.5 0.13 0.5 0.32 0.5 0.13 0.5 0.301 0.5 0.9

ℏ = mP × ℓP × c ℏ = 普朗克质量 × 普朗克长度 × 光速

The reduced Planck constant (ℏ) equals Planck mass times Planck length times the speed of light. This elegant relationship demonstrates how ℏ emerges from other fundamental units. Any civilization can verify this: ℏ = mP · ℓP · c 约化普朗克常数(ℏ)等于普朗克质量乘以普朗克长度乘以光速。 这个优雅的关系展示了ℏ如何从其他基本单位中产生。 任何文明都可以验证：ℏ = mP · ℓP · c

Gravitational Constant

万有引力常数密码本

The Force That Binds Galaxies G = 0.302

Teaching Strategy: G is defined through its relationship with Planck units. 教学策略：G通过与普朗克单位的关系来定义。

0.302 0.5 0.15 0.5 0.32 0.5 0.16 0.5 11 0.5 0.14 0.5 0.33 0.5 0.13 0.5 0.31 0.5 0.16 0.5 10 0.5 0.9

G = ℓP³ ÷ (mP × tP²) G = 普朗克长度³ ÷ (普朗克质量 × 普朗克时间²)

The gravitational constant (G) equals Planck length cubed divided by (Planck mass times Planck time squared). In the encoding: 0.32^3 ÷ (0.33 × 0.31^2). The exponents 3 and 2 are binary 11 and 10. 万有引力常数(G)等于普朗克长度的三次方除以(普朗克质量乘以普朗克时间的平方)。 编码中：0.32^3 ÷ (0.33 × 0.31^2)。指数3和2分别是二进制的11和10。

Why These Constants?

为什么是这些常数？

These three constants — c, G, and ℏ — are the pillars upon which all physics is built. From them, we can derive all Planck units. Any civilization advanced enough to communicate across interstellar distances must have discovered and measured them.

The key insight is that we don't just define these symbols — we prove them through measurable relationships. The electron's Compton wavelength, its mass ratio to Planck mass, and the mathematical relationships between constants are all independently verifiable by any civilization that understands quantum mechanics and general relativity. 这三个常数 — c、G 和 ℏ — 是所有物理学建立的支柱。 从它们，我们可以推导出所有普朗克单位。任何足够先进以进行星际通信的文明 都必须发现并测量了它们。

关键洞察是我们不仅仅定义这些符号——我们通过可测量的关系证明它们。 电子的康普顿波长、它与普朗克质量的质量比、以及常数之间的数学关系 都是任何理解量子力学和广义相对论的文明可以独立验证的。
"The most incomprehensible thing about the universe is that it is comprehensible."
— Albert Einstein

That the universe follows laws — that physics works the same way everywhere — is not obvious. It's a miracle that makes science possible. And it's the foundation of our hope for interstellar communication: if they can understand the universe, they can understand our message.

宇宙遵循规律——物理学在任何地方都以相同的方式运作—— 这并不是显而易见的。这是一个使科学成为可能的奇迹。它是我们对星际通信 希望的基础：如果他们能理解宇宙，他们就能理解我们的信息。
