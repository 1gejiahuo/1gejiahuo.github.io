"Mathematics is the language in which God has written the universe."
— Galileo Galilei

If there is any language that transcends the barriers of biology, culture, and even the vastly different evolutionary paths of alien civilizations, it is mathematics. The relationship between numbers — that 1 + 1 = 2 — is not a human invention. It is a discovery of truth that exists independently of any mind.

如果有任何语言能够跨越生物学、文化甚至外星文明截然不同的进化路径的障碍，那就是数学。 数字之间的关系——1 + 1 = 2——不是人类的发明。它是对独立于任何心智而存在的真理的发现。
