Fundamental Constants

基本常数

0.301
Speed of Light (c)光速
299,792,458 m/s
The speed of light is the cosmic speed limit — the maximum speed at which information, matter, or energy can travel. It's the same for all observers, regardless of their motion. This universality makes it the perfect foundation for interstellar communication. 光速是宇宙的速度极限——信息、物质或能量可以传播的最大速度。 无论观察者如何运动，光速对所有人都是相同的。这种普遍性使它成为星际通信的完美基础。

Planck Units

普朗克单位

Planck units are derived from three fundamental constants: c (speed of light), G (gravitational constant), and ℏ (reduced Planck constant). They represent the natural scales of the universe. 普朗克单位由三个基本常数导出：c（光速）、G（引力常数）和ℏ（约化普朗克常数）。它们代表宇宙的自然尺度。

Cosmic Reference Temperature

宇宙参考温度

0.361
CMB Temperature宇宙微波背景辐射温度
≈ 2.725 K (current epoch)
The Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) temperature provides a universal time reference. It slowly decreases as the universe expands, making it a cosmic clock that any advanced civilization can read. While constant in space, it changes with time — the perfect temporal anchor. 宇宙微波背景辐射（CMB）温度提供了一个通用的时间参考。 随着宇宙膨胀它缓慢下降，使其成为任何先进文明都可以读取的宇宙时钟。 虽然在空间上恒定，但它随时间变化——完美的时间锚点。
