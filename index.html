Our Vision

When We Look Up at the Stars

当我们仰望星空

"We are a way for the cosmos to know itself."

— Carl Sagan

For millennia, humanity has gazed at the night sky with wonder and curiosity. We have always asked: Are we alone? If not, how could we ever communicate with minds utterly different from our own?

千百年来，人类怀着好奇与敬畏仰望星空。我们始终追问：我们是否孤独？ 如果不是，我们如何与完全不同的智慧生命交流？

This project answers that question through electromagnetic waves — the only messenger that can traverse the cosmic void. Binary pulses of light become our alphabet, physics becomes our grammar, and mathematics becomes our shared truth.

这个项目通过电磁波来回答这个问题——这是唯一能穿越宇宙虚空的信使。 光的二进制脉冲成为我们的字母，物理成为我们的语法，数学成为我们共同的真理。

Earth Coordinates

Our Address in the Cosmos

我们在宇宙中的地址

Finding Home Among the Stars

在群星中找到家园

How do you tell someone where you live when there are no shared street names, no common maps? You use the universe's own landmarks — pulsars, those cosmic lighthouses that spin with clockwork precision.

当没有共同的街道名称、没有通用的地图时，你如何告诉别人你住在哪里？ 你使用宇宙自己的地标——脉冲星，那些以精确如钟表般旋转的宇宙灯塔。

15 pulsars, each with a unique rotation period and distance, triangulate Earth's position. And for time? The Cosmic Microwave Background — the afterglow of the Big Bang — provides a universal clock, slowly cooling as the universe ages.

15颗脉冲星，每颗都有独特的旋转周期和距离，三角定位出地球的位置。 那时间呢？宇宙微波背景辐射——大爆炸的余辉—— 提供了一个宇宙时钟，随着宇宙衰老而缓慢冷却。

Mathematics: The Universal Truth

数学：普遍真理

1 + 1 = 2 everywhere in the universe. This simple equation is our bridge across the stars — the first handshake between civilizations.

在宇宙的任何地方，1 + 1 = 2。这个简单的等式是我们跨越星际的桥梁——文明间的第一次握手。

Physics: The Shared Experience

物理：共同经验

Light travels at the same speed for all observers. Gravity bends spacetime the same way everywhere. These laws bind all intelligent beings.

光对所有观察者以相同速度传播。引力在任何地方都以相同方式弯曲时空。这些定律连接所有智慧生命。

Matter: The Common Ancestry

物质：共同起源

Every atom in our bodies was forged in the heart of a star. We are literally made of stardust — as is any life that might be listening.

我们身体中的每一个原子都是在恒星核心中锻造的。我们确实由星尘构成——任何可能在倾听的生命也是如此。
